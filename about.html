About Us

At Mallow Boxing Club, we believe boxing is for everyone. Whether you're stepping into a gym for the first time, looking to improve your fitness, or preparing to compete in the ring, our club provides a welcoming and supportive environment for all ages and skill levels.

Based in Mallow, we offer structured training for children, teenagers, and adults, helping members develop confidence, discipline, fitness, and boxing technique. Our experienced coaches work with both recreational and competitive boxers, ensuring every member can progress at their own pace and achieve their personal goals.

From learning the fundamentals to preparing for competition, Mallow Boxing Club is committed to fostering a positive community where hard work, respect, and determination are at the core of everything we do.

Join us and become part of a proud local boxing tradition.

Our Staff

Head Coach

Bio:

Our Head Coach leads the club with years of experience in competitive boxing and athlete development. He is responsible for overseeing all training programs, refining technique, and ensuring every boxer trains with discipline, structure, and purpose. His focus is on building fighters who are technically sound, physically prepared, and mentally resilient.

Coach 1

Bio:

Coach 1 specializes in technical development and pad work, focusing on refining fundamentals such as footwork, timing, and defensive movement. He works closely with boxers to correct technique and build strong habits that translate directly into performance in the ring.

Coach 2

Bio:

Coach 2 brings a high-energy approach to conditioning and skill development sessions. He emphasizes fitness, intensity, and repetition, helping athletes build endurance, power, and confidence while maintaining strong boxing fundamentals throughout their training.
